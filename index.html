Our Foundations

The Pillars of KV Excellence

We design our programs around three core areas aligned with KVS values — academic rigour, character building, and national integration.

Academic Excellence

CBSE curriculum with NCERT-based teaching, STEM labs, and PM SHRI innovation modules to foster critical thinking and scientific temper.

Character & Values

Integrity, discipline, and social responsibility are woven into daily school life. We build compassionate citizens ready to serve the nation.

National Integration

Through Hindi-English bilingual education, cultural programmes, and KVS national events, students learn unity in diversity.

Why Our KV?

PMSHRI Kendriya Vidyalaya goes beyond textbooks. We integrate smart classrooms, hands-on labs, and personalised guidance for every learner.
