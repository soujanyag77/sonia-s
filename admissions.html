Path to Enrollment

The Admission Process

Transparent and fair admission process following KVS priority categories and guidelines.

1

Online Registration

Register on the official KVS admission portal during the notified admission window.

2

Document Verification

Submit required documents at school for category-wise verification as per KVS norms.

3

Lottery / Merit List

Selection through lottery or merit list based on category and available seats.

4

Fee Payment & Confirmation

Complete fee payment and medical form submission to confirm admission.
