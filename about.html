Our Journey

A History of Excellence

Since our founding, Apex Academy has evolved from a small local school into a premier regional institution.

2005

The Foundation

Apex Academy was established with a single building, 12 staff members, and 120 primary school students, committed to active learning models.

2012

High School Expansion

Opened the senior secondary campus, introducing Advanced Placement (AP) courses and building the first dedicated STEM workshop facility.

2018

Athletic & Arts Complex

Completed construction on our Olympic-sized indoor pool, performance hall, and graphic art studios, broadening extracurricular horizons.

2024

Digital & Eco Initiative

Achieved carbon-neutral status for all buildings and updated curriculum to feature integrated AI studies and renewable systems modules.
